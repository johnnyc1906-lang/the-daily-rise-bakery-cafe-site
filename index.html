Our Story

A Labor of Love Since Day One

Welcome to The Daily Rise Bakery & Cafe, Sandy Hook's beloved neighborhood spot where the aroma of freshly baked bread and expertly brewed coffee greets you every morning. We rise early so you can start your day right.

From our signature sourdough loaves to delicate croissants, every item is crafted with care using locally sourced ingredients. Our cafe offers a warm, welcoming atmosphere perfect for catching up with friends, working remotely, or simply savoring a quiet morning moment.

With a near-perfect 4.9-star rating from our beloved community, we take immense pride in the quality and consistency that keeps our regulars coming back every single day.
